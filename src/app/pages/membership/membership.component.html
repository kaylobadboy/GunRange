Membership Info

  • Shooting Facilities

  • 25, 50, 100, 200, and 300 yard shooting ranges with soil back stops
  • Covered bench rests 50, 100, 200 & 300
  • International Trap shooting on designated nights
  • New club house built in 1996 with kitchen facilities
  • Shooting events throughout the shooting season
  • Located in a woodland setting just 12 miles from town
  • Open all year for target practice

  • Membership Prices

  • Annual Individual Membership Dues - $85
  • Family Membership - $100
  • A Family Membership includes yourself, your spouse and any dependent children (under 18), no guests.
  • New Member Initiation Fee - $25
  • Guest Membership $25/day
  • Memberships are through December 31st.
  • An EGF Rod & Gun Club membership entitles you to use of the shooting ranges and manual trap range.
