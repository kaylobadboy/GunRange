    Outdoor Traditions Since 1927

    "The purpose of the East Grand Forks Rod & Gun Club shall be to promote an interest in sound conservation practices, to support improvement in game and fish habitat. It is also our objective to promote sage handling, improved marksmanship, proper care and use of firearms. Further, it is also our objective to encourage the development of the characteristics of honesty, good fellowship, responsible self-discipline, team play and self-reliance. These ideals are the essentials of good sportsmanship and the foundation of patriotism."
